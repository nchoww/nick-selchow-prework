About Me

Nick Selchow grew up in Apple Valley, Minnesota and graduated from Eastview High School in 2013. He attended Stevens Institute of Technology in Hoboken, New Jersey for one year and studied computer engineering. He transferred to the University of Minnesota Twin Cities his sophomore year to pursue a degree in computer science. In late 2016, he completed his degree and graduated from the U of M.
Since graduating, Nick has worked at the Minneapolis Club as a fitness center attendant and at Prepworks as a content developer. In both of these positions, he was able to use the skills gained while pursuing his degree to develop programs that increased efficiency in some aspect.
In his free time, Nick enjoys spending time outdoors in any capacity. In particular, he enjoys camping in the Boundary Waters Canoe Area, located in northern Minnesota. When he is not coding or camping, you can find Nick playing piano, traveling, and spending time with his two Brittany Spaniels, Gracie and Sydney.
